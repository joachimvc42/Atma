Atma Lombok

Atma Lombok is a social art project that turns recycled wood and ocean‑inspired creativity into colourful boards. Each piece helps keep the island clean and supports children from low‑income families to go to school.

The women of the village

Under the shade of thatched roofs, laughter fills the air as the women of the village sit together, shaping stories with their hands.

They thread ocean‑polished shells, shimmering beads and small treasures collected over time, transforming them into bracelets and necklaces that carry the soul of Lombok.

Each bracelet is more than a piece of jewelry: it is a memory you can wear, a fragment of their daily life, and a little piece of their joy, warmth and spirit that travels with you.

The wood craftsman

In a quiet workshop, the soft sound of carving wood fills the space. A local craftsman patiently shapes each board, following the natural lines and imperfections of the wood.

On these wooden canvases, we paint waves, palm trees and fragments of island life. Every colour, every brushstroke is a tribute to Lombok, to its people and to the sea that surrounds them.

Each finished board is unique. It tells a story of patient hands, recycled materials given a second life, and a community that believes creativity can be a real engine for change.

Solidarity initiatives

When you buy a board, you are not just taking home a beautiful object. You are helping to fund educational projects, waste‑collection initiatives and local environmental actions on the island.

A part of each sale sends children to school, supports families in difficulty and contributes to keeping Lombok’s beaches clean, its oceans blue and its villages thriving.

Every purchase becomes a gesture of solidarity: a choice to believe that the future is built not only with what we create, but also with what we choose to give back.
